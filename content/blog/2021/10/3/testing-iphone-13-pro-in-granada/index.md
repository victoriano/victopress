<div class="sqs-html-content" data-sqsp-text-block-content>
  <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">I have to say, I am <strong>pretty amazed by the results</strong>,  after using an iPhone XS for the past 3 years. The major improvements with respect to my previous iPhone are:</p><ul data-rte-list="default"><li><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">The <strong>3X optical zoom</strong>. Most of my best street photography pictures with my DSLR are taken with 24-105mm lenses. The 3X optical zoom is equivalent to a 77mm lens. I found myself using it all the time to bring elements closer in city landscapes. Like cars and trees with buildings.</p></li><li><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">The new <strong>0.5X wide-angle lens</strong>, equivalent to 13mm, is also handy for some types of images you can really magnify a person, animal, or element to respect their surroundings. </p></li></ul>

<div class="sqs-html-content" data-sqsp-text-block-content>
  <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">There is also<a href="https://www.tomsguide.com/news/iphone-13-pro-macro-camera-is-so-good-that-a-doctors-using-it-for-this" target=""><strong> a new macro lens</strong></a> that is amazing too, but I’ll probably use it only from time to time to capture insects, leaves, and other beautiful stuff, but definitely not my primary focus on taking pictures. I feel like it’s difficult to be original with macro photography and small creatures are pretty similar… 😂</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">But one of my biggest and unexpected surprises has been the new <strong>Apple ProRAW</strong> format. It was introduced at the end of last year for the iPhone 12. After testing it using <strong>VSCO</strong> I am really happy with how much control I get on the highlights and shadows, pretty similar to the experience with <strong>Adobe Camera RAW</strong> and the RAW files from my DSLRs <strong>Canon</strong> and <strong>Sony</strong>. As you can see in the images above and below the dynamic range in this difficult light situation is astonishing 🤯 . Read the <a href="https://austinmann.com/trek/iphone-13-pro-camera-review-tanzania" target="">Austin Mann review</a> to learn more about the video capabilities.  </p>
